Ooni unveils sixth wife, Olori Akinmuda

Olaiya Champion
June 29,2023 - 10:14

The Ooni of Ife, Oba Adeyeye Enitan Ogunwusi, has unveiled his his wife, Opeoluwa Elizabeth Akinmuda.
According to reports, the monarch, who took his sixth wife sometime in May, is set to officially welcome her into his Royal Palace of Oodua in Ile-Ife The invitations for the Thanksgiving service and reception of the new queen have reportedly been sent out. Top dignitaries are expected to attend this grand event.

The event is scheduled to hold on Saturday 20th May 2023.

The couple reportedly tied the knot on May 10th, 2023.

Meanwhile, the palace has yet to comment on the development as of filing the report.

Recall that Oba Enitan Adeyeye, is already married to five other women namely; Olori Mariam, Olori Tobi Phillips Ogunwusi, Princess Ashley Adegoke, Princess Ronke Ademiluyi and Temitope Adesegun.
